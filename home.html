About AZMUN 2025

AZMUN 2025 brings together young minds from across the globe to engage in meaningful dialogue and debate on pressing international issues. Our conference provides a platform for delegates to develop their diplomatic skills, critical thinking, and understanding of global affairs.

Volunteer Opportunities

Our Committees

International Court of Justice (ICJ)

Topic: Turkey v. Israel: Alleged Violations of Maritime and Humanitarian Law in the 2024 Gaza Blockade

This committee will analyze the legality of Israel's recent military actions under international law. Delegates will argue cases on war crimes, sovereignty disputes, and humanitarian obligations.

United Nations General Assembly (UNGA)

Topic: Regulating AI in Warfare and Autonomous Weapons Systems

As AI technology advances, the world faces ethical and security challenges. This session will discuss policies to govern autonomous weapons and prevent misuse of AI in military conflicts.
