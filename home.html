About AZMUN 2025

AZMUN 2025 brings together young minds from across the globe to engage in meaningful dialogue and debate on pressing international issues. Our conference provides a platform for delegates to develop their diplomatic skills, critical thinking, and understanding of global affairs.

Our Committees

UNGA

United Nations General Assembly - Addressing global challenges and international cooperation.

UNHRC

United Nations Human Rights Council - Promoting and protecting human rights worldwide.

UNSC

United Nations Security Council - Maintaining international peace and security.
