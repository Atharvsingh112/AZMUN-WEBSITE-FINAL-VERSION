About Ayoniz Model United Nations

Our Mission

AZMUN is a premier Model United Nations conference that brings together young leaders from across the globe to engage in meaningful debate, diplomacy, and policy-making. Our goal is to empower youth voices in solving the world's most pressing issues through innovative simulations and real-world diplomacy.

Our Founder

Atharv Singh

Founder & Secretary General

Atharv Singh dreams of AZMUN as more than just another Model United Nations—it is going to be a vibrant space where youth engage with global issues, build diplomacy skills, and make meaningful contributions to actual policy discussions.

Aside from his passion for international relations and policymaking, Atharv is keenly interested in medical sciences, with a deep desire to understand how health policies shape society. To him, both diplomacy and science are interlinked. They require critical thinking, research, and solving complex problems to bring real change to the world.

From competitive debate to global policy forums, bridging this gap has become Atharv's vision. He sees AZMUN as a space for future leaders to grapple with pressing global challenges—from healthcare crises to geopolitical tensions—working toward actionable solutions.

In Atharv's understanding, AZMUN hopes to inspire an entire generation of changemakers—students who are not only skillfully trained in the art of diplomacy but also prepared to tackle challenges at the intersection of health, technology, and global governance.
